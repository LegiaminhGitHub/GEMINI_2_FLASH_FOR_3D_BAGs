How It Works

Learn how easy it is to recycle your clothes and get rewarded.

1. Deposit Your Clothes

Bring your old, used clothing to a designated Whaleconomist machine.

2. Receive Vouchers

Our machine assesses your deposit, and you instantly receive valuable vouchers.

3. Reimagine the Fabric

The fabrics from your old clothes are extracted and used to create our sustainable tote bags.
